Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Controls

  • Up Arrow - drive forward
  • Down Arrow - drive backward
  • Left Arrow - turn left
  • Right Arrow - turn right
  • O - open/close doors - CLOSED
  • K - toggle point light - ON
  • L - toggle directional light - ON
  • T - toggle floor texture - OFF

Please note: you can hold down a key to continually move/turn, etc.
Also, while point and directional lighting can be viewed together, they are clearer one at a time,
especially while turning on the spot. No ambient lighting is included.
